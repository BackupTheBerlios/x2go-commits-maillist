====== X2Go packages for SUSE-based systems ======

== Add the repository ==

<code bash>
zypper ar obs://X11:RemoteDesktop:x2go/openSUSE_12.3 x2go
</code>

Substitute ''openSUSE_12.3'' by the tag for the distribution you are actually using. Available ones are: ''openSUSE_Factory'', ''openSUSE_12.2'', ''openSUSE_12.3'' and ''SLE_11'' (SLES 11 SP2).

Congratulations, you are now able to access the X2Go packages. You may continue by installing ''x2goserver'', ''x2goclient'' or ''pyhoca-gui'' or any other of the available packages.

<code bash>
zypper in
x2goclient
</code>
